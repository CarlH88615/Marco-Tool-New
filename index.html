💾 Backup Guide

🚨 IMPORTANT: Before any app update, always backup your macros!
1
Export Your Macros
Click the "💾 Export" button to download a backup file. This saves all your macros, usage statistics, and settings to your computer.
2
Save the File Safely
Save the downloaded JSON file somewhere you'll remember (like your Desktop or Documents folder). The filename includes today's date for easy identification.
3
After Updates
When the app is updated and your macros are gone, click "📁 Import" and select your backup file. You can choose to merge with any existing macros or replace everything.
